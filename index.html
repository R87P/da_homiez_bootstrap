DA HOMIEZ

Da Homiez Food Trucc revolves around the idea of providing pure and simple fresh food, made from real ingredients. Serving snacks, salads, sandwiches, and meals, you can trust that this is not your ordinary fast food. Anything can be customized to fit your dietary needs and pallet, making it perfect for everyone. Come try our healthy spin on street food today!

Our History

Da Homiez food trucc came about when Marc Marquez and Valentino Rossi had the idea to satisfy Austin, Texas' growing appetite for Healthy Great tasting food While on their way to work one day, the couple brainstormed on how they could bring new flavors and tastes to Austin. "We always wanted to do our own concept," says Rossi. "We weren't open to working under someone else's brand; we wanted to start our own business."

"There are a lot of people involved in Da Homiez concept. It takes a lot of time to make great food and pay attention to detail. A lot of us have been friends for a long time, which made it easy to get the right people on board with the concept."
